Leak Detection

Non-Invasive Tech

Hidden leaks can silently destroy your home's structure and rack up your water bill for months before they're noticed. Our advanced, non-invasive leak detection technology pinpoints leaks without tearing out walls or floors.

We use acoustic listening devices, thermal imaging, and moisture meters to locate leaks behind walls, under slabs, and underground.

  • Acoustic & electronic leak detection
  • Thermal imaging camera inspection
  • Slab leak detection & repair
  • Underground water main leak location
  • Pool & irrigation system leak detection
  • Water meter & bill audit
  • Post-repair verification testing
